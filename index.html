Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla is someone I admire deeply because of his incredible contributions to science and technology.

Early Life

Nikola Tesla was born on July 10, 1856, in Smiljan, Croatia. He showed an early interest in electricity and pursued studies in engineering and physics.

Achievements

Tesla is best known for his contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electrical systems, wireless transmission, and many other groundbreaking inventions.

Legacy

His legacy lives on through the numerous technologies and scientific principles that continue to shape our modern world.
